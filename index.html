about

My name is Kelby Kunz! I am a 200 RYT certified yoga instructor, dedicated to integrating mindfulness, creativity and self-love to the practice of yoga.

I have been regularly practicing yoga since 2016, and was first drawn to yoga as I found it provided balance in mind, body and spirit. I enjoyed the physical practice and challenge that asana can bring, all while loving the mental and emotional clarity that came through its meditative nature. I completed my 200-hour certification with Ashley Colloton Yoga through Mountainside fitness in Phoenix, AZ, in May 2017 and have been teaching ever since.

I am trained in and instruct several styles of yoga including, Ashtanga, Vinyasa Flow, All Levels, Beginner, Power, Yin, and Restorative. I strive to bring encouragement, compassion, creativity, and intention into all of my classes and feel a true joy in guiding others to a greater sense of peace through yoga.

Growing up in the Pacific Northwest instilled a love for camping, hiking, and all things outdoors. I have an undeniable sweet tooth and a love for learning. Currently, I am located in Phoenix, AZ with my husband, daughter and chocolate lab.

My passion is to empower students to be their best self, live authentically, and find peace and contentment.
